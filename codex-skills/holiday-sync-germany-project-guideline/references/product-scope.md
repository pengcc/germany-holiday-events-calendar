# Product Scope

## Purpose

Holiday Sync Germany helps Chinese-speaking families and travel planners in Germany compare German public holidays and school holidays across federal states. The product should highlight overlapping holiday periods so users can plan travel and avoid peak periods.

## Target Users

- Chinese-speaking families living in Germany.
- Travel planners comparing school and public holidays across German states.
- Secondary audience: German and English readers who need the same comparison experience.

## First Release Scope

- Static web app with `/zh`, `/de`, and `/en` routes.
- Coverage goal: all 16 German federal states for current and next year.
- Compare two or more states.
- Show public holidays, school holiday periods, and overlapping dates.
- Use a polished calendar/heatmap comparison experience.

## Explicitly Out Of Scope

- Accounts, authentication, saved trips, alerts, reminders, personal data, and profiles.
- Calendar subscription/export until explicitly planned later.
- Travel booking, recommendations requiring personal data, or affiliate flows.
- Backend runtime services and paid APIs.
- Analytics or telemetry without explicit confirmation.
